Truck Loads - Driver Logistics App

About the Application

Take control of your trucking business with Truck Loads, the complete logistics management tool designed for owner-operators and professional truck drivers. This application digitizes your entire workflow, from managing loads and tracking expenses to generating professional invoices and settlement reports, all from your Android device.

This app was developed as a capstone project for a software engineering program, showcasing a full-stack mobile application built with .NET MAUI, C#, and SQLite for a robust, offline-first experience.
